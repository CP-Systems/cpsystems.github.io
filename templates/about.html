About Us

Discover our inspiring journey and mission to revolutionize water management and leak detection in Africa through IoT innovation.

Our Mission

CP Systems is committed to building scalable, sensor-based water monitoring platforms for African communities. With our AquaSense solution, we enable utilities to detect leaks, monitor flow, and improve water delivery using affordable technology and real-time dashboards.

Our Technology

We combine edge processing, NRF24/LoRa radios, solar-powered nodes, GSM gateways, and cloud databases like InfluxDB to deliver real-time insights into water flow, anomalies, and leakages.

Where We Work

We are proudly based in Kigali, Rwanda, and actively deploy smart water infrastructure in collaboration with WASAC and international research partners.
